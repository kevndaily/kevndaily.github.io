My name is Kevin and I am a badminton player from Ireland. With the Olympics coming up this year I thought I would make a site to share the details of the best 50 players in mens and women singles and show their form since the last Olympics. In the end I will predict who I think will win the gold medal in both events. I have more plans for this site but I wanted to get this much up for now.


You will notice that there are one or two players missing from each table acccording to the rankings.Some players have retired but are still on rankings so I have accounted for this.

My Predictions

Men's Singles: Kento Momota


Kento Momota has been the most dominant player since the last Olympics. In 2019 he won 11 titles in the season, the most ever in mens singles. With the Olympics being in Japan I believe that also gives him an advantage. He has won the Japan Open twice since 2016 so he doesn't fall under the pressure of the home fans. I don't see anyone beating him here.


Women's Singles: Carolina Marin


Most people probably would have expected me to go with Tai Tzu Ying as she has been the best player since the last Olympics and as it is her last tournament before retirement she will give it absolutely everything. However, I cannot ignore the fact she has always underperformed at the Japan Open and the World Championships, the biggest tournament of each year. So, instead I am going with the reigning winner Carolina Marin. She is familiar with winning in Japan and she knows what it takes to win an Olympics. Her only problem will be staying fit as she has been injured quite a bit recently.
